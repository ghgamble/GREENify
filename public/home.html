Are you curious about how to be more eco-friendly but are intimidated by the steps that you need to take in order to acheive your goal? This competition was designed to address these needs by turning the process into a game! The hope is that with each passing day, you are able to take one small step and challenge yourself to complete the tasks within 30 days. Each time you complete a core task, you will receive 20 points. You will receive 5 points for each daily task reminder prompt. Challenge your friends!
